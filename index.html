About KNK Mattress

Founded in 1999, KNK Mattress is a trusted name in handcrafted mattress manufacturing, proudly rooted in tradition and quality.

We specialize in handmade mattresses crafted from natural kapok fiber, known for its breathability, resilience, and eco-friendly properties. Each mattress is carefully made by skilled artisans to ensure lasting comfort and durability.

Our mission is to deliver naturally comfortable, sustainable, and long-lasting sleep solutions while preserving the craftsmanship that defines our heritage.
